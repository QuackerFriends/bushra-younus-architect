About the studio

Design that
begins with listening

Every project starts with a conversation. I believe the best architecture doesn't impose itself on a site — it emerges from understanding who lives there, how light moves, what history is held in the ground beneath.

With over a decade of practice across residential homes, commercial interiors, and community spaces, I approach every brief with equal attention to the structural and the poetic.

Based in North Carolina, working across the US and internationally.

What I offer

Full-spectrum design services

01

Residential Architecture

Custom homes, extensions, and renovations tailored entirely around how you want to live. From concept to construction drawings.

02

Commercial Design

Offices, retail spaces, hospitality venues. Environments that work for the people in them and communicate your brand clearly.

03

Interior Architecture

Spatial planning, material selection, lighting design. The inside matters just as much as the facade.

04

Planning & Permits

Navigating local planning regulations, permit applications, and council submissions so you don't have to.

05

3D Visualization

Photorealistic renders and walkthroughs before a single wall goes up. See your project in full before committing.

06

Sustainable Design

Low-impact materials, passive solar strategies, green certifications. Building better for now and for later.

How it works

The design process

01

Discovery

We meet, we listen, we ask the right questions. Understanding you and your site is the foundation everything else is built on.

02

Concept

Sketches, moodboards, initial spatial ideas. We explore multiple directions before locking in on the one that feels right.

03

Development

Detailed drawings, 3D models, materials, costings. Every decision is made together and documented clearly.

04

Delivery

On-site presence, contractor coordination, quality checks. The job isn't done until the space is exactly as intended.
